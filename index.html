Privacy Policy for Vyapo App

Effective Date: 26 January 2026

Vyapo App (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our eCommerce mobile application (“App”). This policy is created to comply with Google Play Store requirements.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Shipping and Billing Address
  • Login credentials (encrypted)

1.2 Order & Transaction Information

  • Products viewed and purchased
  • Order history
  • Payment status (we do not store card or UPI details)

1.3 Device & Technical Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • App version
  • IP address (for security and analytics)
  • Crash logs and performance data

1.4 Optional Permissions

  • Camera (for profile image or product uploads, if applicable)
  • Storage (for uploading images or documents)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process orders and deliveries
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send order updates and important notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Payment Information

All payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. Vyapo App does not store your debit card, credit card, UPI, or net banking details.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share limited information only with:

  • Delivery partners (for order fulfillment)
  • Payment gateway providers
  • Government or legal authorities, if required by law

All third parties are required to follow strict data protection practices.

5. Data Security

  • Secure APIs
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Restricted access to user information

However, no digital platform is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

7. User Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw consent for optional permissions

8. Children’s Privacy

Vyapo App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
